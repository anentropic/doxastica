<objective>
Implement `get_scope_at` (HIST-03) — the temporal-reconstruction read method on `MemoryCore` — as a near-clone of the shipped `query_scope` body, with the ONE structural change that defines time-travel: an inclusive `source_event_id <= as_of` CUT applied to the candidate states BEFORE the per-belief ordering-max (cut-then-max = REWIND), in place of `query_scope`'s `event_id_max` POST-filter (max-then-filter = DROP). This plan lands the Wave-0 failing example tests first (the local spec / regression guard for the central trap), then makes them green.

Purpose: HIST-03 is the last unimplemented `BeliefStore` method and the structural foundation NVM's CQRS event-sourcing replay story rests on (`get_scope_at(guard, t0)` = "what did the guard believe at T0"; `get_scope_at(world, e42)` = "what was true before the sale"). The cut-then-max-vs-max-then-cut distinction is the single bug the whole phase exists to avoid.

Output: `MemoryCore.get_scope_at` body in `src/doxastica/core.py`; `tests/test_scope_at.py` (example-test half — the operational-fold property is plan 06-02).
</objective>
